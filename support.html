Support Our Work

Your contribution helps us continue organizing events and programs that celebrate Black history and build a more equitable Whatcom County.

How to Donate

The Bellingham Unity Committee's fiscal sponsor is the Whatcom Human Rights Task Force, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Contributions are tax-deductible.

To donate, visit the Whatcom Human Rights Task Force donation page and note Bellingham Unity Committee as the intended recipient in the memo or comments field.
