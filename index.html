Our Mission

Bellingham Unity Committee organizes events, celebrations, and programs in Whatcom County that commemorate and honor Black History and the rich legacy of African American culture to build a more equitable & inclusive community.

Celebration is Our Spirit

Annual events including Juneteenth celebrations, Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Unity Balls, and the 2020 Peaceful Solidarity Rally provide opportunities for community education, connection, and celebration of history, family, music, and movements.

Community is Our Heart

We are dedicated to building an inclusive local society through partnerships with organizations across Whatcom County — from education and faith communities to arts, advocacy, and student groups.

Education is Our Future

We offer community outreach, education, and training programs on diversity, equity, and inclusion for workplaces and community groups throughout the region.
